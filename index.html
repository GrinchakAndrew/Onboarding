Probation Completion Terms and Conditions

Probation Duration

  • Probation extension may be granted towards achieving required standards; however, a failure to do so could result in termination of employment.

Salary Info
  • Salary is paid on a monthly basis, consisting of flat rate.
  • As your salary has been outlined in your Job Offer, any other amendments to your salary will be mailed to you by the Company.
  • If you have any concerns regarding your paycheck, please address Ann Golub.
  • Unless agreed otherwise, any payment errors, whether overdraft or underpayment, will be rectified with the following salary payment(s).
  • Maxymiser provides labor market surveys and reviews salaries according to the survey data twice a year.
  • Salary is to be revised on the basis of such a review.
  • Maxymiser Salaries are accounted by the Inter-Bank Currency Rates of Ukraine current rates of US Dollars exchange.

Attendance and Notification of Absence
  • Maxymiser values your presence at work and is committed to improving the general well-being of its employees. We do not expect employees to report for work when they are unwell.
  • Team Lead should be notified as soon as possible, preferably by the beginning of the working day, in case of anticipated absence from work, hospitalization, or any other medical treatment.
  • During prolonged periods of absence, your Manager should be kept informed of progress and an expected date of your return.

Days off with Work-off
  • You can take a day off with compensatory work-off day on the weekend.
  • The work-off day should take place during the same month as the day off taken.
  • Also, you can take a non-refundable day off, which is not paid for at the end of the month.

Vacation
  • Please make sure you sign in to 'Activ Absence' online tracking program to mark your leave, respectively.
  • Maxymiser employees are entitled to 18 paid working days-off as annual leave that amount to 24 calendar days in Ukraine, according to Ukrainian Labor Code.
  • Vacation days can be taken on a half-a-year basis.
  • Generally, no more than 10 days-off are up for the taking (i.e., 2 weeks) at a time; exceptional cases should be arranged with the manager.
  • Vacation time payment is calculated on the basis of your average salary for the past 6 months of work (excluding bonuses, extra-hours or overtimes).
  • If you have unused vacation days leftover from the previous year, the Company will provide the compensation for those at the beginning of the next year.
  • Annual leaves must be agreed upon with your manager in advance.
  • Corresponding vacation ticket is to be filled post-factum in the Open Air, and advance planning should be marked in the 'Active Absence' system, accordingly.

  • Please, observe the following rules for vacation planning:

    • Vacation should be coordinated with the working availability plans of the other colleagues.

    • There should be no less than 60% of colleagues available at work at any given time.

    • Proportion of JS Dev & QC availability should be equal.

    • Team Leader and Technical Analyst must not be out of work simultaneously.

    • Before vacation time (2 weeks in advance) each colleague should write up a substitution plan (replacement in case of urgent issues), and confirm this replacement plan with the Team Lead.

    • It is also necessary to activate automatic responder in the mail client (Microsoft Outlook, Menu “File”, “Automatic Replies”).

  • Continual vacation duration should not exceed 2 weeks.
Holidays
  • Please make sure you sign in to 'Activ Absence' online tracking program to mark your leave, respectively.
  • In Maxymiser Ukraine, all of the UA national holidays are non-working.
  • National holidays are covered by employee current salary base.
  • If a national holiday falls on a weekend, it's to be transferred to the next working day, taking into account UA Code of Labor.
  • Corresponding vacation ticket is to be filled post-factum in the Open Air, and advance planning should be marked in the 'Active Absence' system, accordingly.

Sick Leaves
  • Please make sure you sign in to 'Activ Absence' online tracking program to mark your leave, respectively.
  • Company provides coverage for up to 5 days on a sick-leave per year.
  • Sick leaves are granted after passing the trial.
  • Such days are covered by employee's current salary base.
  • Sick-list is preferable in case employee is absent during more than 3 days in a row(subject to the discretion of the Line Manager).
  • Corresponding vacation ticket is to be filled post-factum in the Open Air, and advance planning should be marked in the 'Active Absence' system, accordingly.
